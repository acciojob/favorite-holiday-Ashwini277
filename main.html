Paris


Paris is the capital city of France, known for its art, culture, and history. It is often called the "City of Light" and is famous for its iconic landmarks and delicious cuisine.

  1. Eiffel Tower: The most famous landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum: Home to the Mona Lisa and many other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral: A stunning example of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées: A famous avenue known for its theatres, cafés, and luxury shops.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica: Located at the highest point in the city.
  • Baguette: A staple of French cuisine.
  • Crêpes: Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
  • Escargot: A traditional French dish made with snails.
  • Macarons: Delicate and colorful meringue-based cookies.
  • Ratatouille: A vegetable stew originating from Provence.
