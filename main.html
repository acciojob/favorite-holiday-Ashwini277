Bengaluru

Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is a vibrant city located in southern India. It is renowned for its pleasant climate, lush green spaces, and dynamic culture. Known as the "Silicon Valley of India," Bengaluru is a major tech hub with a rich heritage and numerous attractions.


  1. Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace - A beautiful historical site showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture.
  2. Lalbagh Botanical Garden - A sprawling garden with a diverse collection of plants and a historic glasshouse.
  3. Vidhana Soudha - The impressive legislative building with stunning neo-Dravidian architecture.
  4. Bangalore Palace - A majestic palace inspired by England's Windsor Castle.
  5. Nandi Hills - A scenic hill station offering breathtaking views and a serene environment.
  • Masala Dosa - A crispy rice pancake filled with spicy potatoes.
  • Biryani - A flavorful rice dish with aromatic spices and tender meat or vegetables.
  • Filter Coffee - A strong and aromatic coffee brewed using a traditional filter.
  • Idli and Sambar - Steamed rice cakes served with a tangy lentil soup.
  • Vada - Deep-fried lentil doughnuts with a crispy texture.
